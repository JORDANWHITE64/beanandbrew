Whats On

Check out our upcoming events and activities!

Live Music Night

Join us every Friday night for live music performances by local artists. Enjoy great coffee and a vibrant atmosphere while listening to talented musicians.

Date: Every Friday

Time: 7:00 PM - 10:00 PM

Art Exhibition

Experience the creativity of local artists at our monthly art exhibition. We showcase a variety of artworks, including paintings, sculptures, and photography. Come and support the local art scene while enjoying your favorite coffee.

Date: First Saturday of every month

Time: 10:00 AM - 4:00 PM

Book Club

Join our monthly book club where we discuss a selected book over coffee. It's a great opportunity to connect with fellow book lovers and share your thoughts on the latest reads.

Date: Last Wednesday of every month

Time: 6:00 PM - 8:00 PM

Barista Workshops

Learn the art of coffee making with our barista workshops. Our experienced baristas will teach you how to brew the perfect cup of coffee, from espresso to latte art. Whether you're a beginner or an enthusiast, these workshops are designed to enhance your coffee knowledge and skills.

Date: Every second Saturday

Time: 2:00 PM - 4:00 PM

Community Open Mic Night

Showcase your talent or simply enjoy the performances at our community open mic night . Whether you're a musician, poet, comedian, or storyteller, this is your chance to share your creativity with the community. Sign up at the counter to secure your spot on the stage.

Date: Every third Thursday

Time: 7:00 PM - 10:00 PM
